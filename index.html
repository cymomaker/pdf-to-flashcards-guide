What is PDF to flashcards?

PDF to flashcards is a study workflow that converts source material from PDFs into reviewable cards. For Anki users, the best output is usually an APKG deck that can be imported into Anki Desktop, AnkiMobile, or AnkiDroid.

When PDF to flashcards works well

Lecture notes

Convert definitions, formulas, and important facts from class notes into active recall prompts.

Textbook chapters

Turn dense explanations into smaller Q&A or Cloze cards that are easier to review every day.

Research papers

Extract key claims, methods, limitations, and terminology from academic PDFs.

Cloze vs Q&A cards

Cloze cards are useful when you want to hide a key phrase inside a sentence. Q&A cards are better when a concept can be tested with a direct question. A strong Anki deck often uses both formats.

How to import APKG into Anki

  1. Download the generated APKG deck.
  2. Open Anki Desktop.
  3. Choose File, then Import.
  4. Select the APKG file and confirm the import.
  5. Review and edit cards before serious study.
